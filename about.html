About this dashboard

IDB Assets Dashboard · Powered by the GIS Team, Ikeja Electric

What this is

The IDB Assets Dashboard is a private, internal asset-tracking tool used by the Ikeja Electric IDB (Intelligence Data Box) team to monitor field asset-tagging progress across business units. It is not a public Ikeja Electric service and does not offer accounts to the general public.

Who operates it

It is operated by the GIS Team, Ikeja Electric. The Ikeja Electric name and logo are used with authorization for this internal tool.

Access

Access is by administrator invitation only — there is no public sign-up. Authorized staff sign in with credentials issued by the IDB Dashboard Administrator.
